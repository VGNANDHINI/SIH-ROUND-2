# **App Name**: JalSaathi

## Core Features:

- Role-Based Dashboards: Provide role-specific dashboards for Gram Panchayat Members, Pump Operators, Village Residents, and Block/District Officials.
- Water Scheme Management: Enable Gram Panchayat Members to manage water supply schemes, including adding, updating, and removing schemes.
- Issue Reporting: Allow Pump Operators to report issues related to water pumps, including descriptions, images, and locations.
- Water Availability Checker: Enable Village Residents to check the current water availability in their area.
- Data Analysis Dashboards: Provide Block/District Officials with data analysis dashboards for regional water management, with insights and visualizations.
- Maintenance Scheduling: Enable pump operators/local water technicians to manage maintenance schedules for pumps. The LLM acts as a scheduling tool that takes into account the last completed service, the pump's failure rate, and operating hours to suggest maintenance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light blue (#ADD8E6) to evoke a sense of water and cleanliness.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F8FF) for a clean and fresh look.
- Accent color: Green (#90EE90) to highlight important information and actions.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and a little warmth or personality; suitable for headlines or body text
- Use water-related icons for navigation and actions.
- Use a clean and intuitive layout for easy navigation and data visualization.
- Subtle animations to indicate loading and data updates.
